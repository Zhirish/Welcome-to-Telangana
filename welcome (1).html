Explore Telangana

About Telangana

Telangana is one of the fastest-growing states in India, known for its rich history, vibrant traditions and technological growth. The capital city Hyderabad is globally famous for IT industries, historical monuments and world-famous Hyderabadi Biryani.

The state has a glorious cultural heritage shaped by powerful dynasties like the Kakatiyas and Qutb Shahis. Ancient temples, forts and palaces reflect Telangana’s artistic excellence.

Telangana is also known for handloom traditions, folk dances, music and colorful festivals like Bathukamma and Bonalu. The state attracts tourists from around the world with its unique blend of modern lifestyle and historical beauty.

Culture & Festivals

Bathukamma, Bonalu and Sammakka Saralamma Jatara are among the most celebrated festivals in Telangana. Traditional arts like Perini dance, Bidri craft, Pochampally sarees and Telangana folk music showcase the state's cultural richness.

Top Tourist Places in Telangana

Charminar

The iconic monument of Hyderabad built in 1591, famous for its Indo-Islamic architecture.

Golconda Fort

A magnificent hill fort known for its royal history, acoustic system and stunning city views.

Ramappa Temple

A UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its extraordinary Kakatiya-style architecture.

Falaknuma Palace

A luxurious palace famous for its royal architecture, beautiful interiors and historical importance.

Qutb Shahi Tombs

A historic temple famous for its detailed carvings and beautiful stone pillars.

Thousand pillar Temple

A historic temple famous for its detailed carvings and beautiful stone pillars.
